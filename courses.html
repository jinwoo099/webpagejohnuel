Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)

The BSCS program provides a strong foundation in programming, software development, algorithms, data structures, and systems analysis. Students will gain hands-on experience in web development, database management, and emerging technologies like artificial intelligence and cybersecurity. Graduates are prepared to become software engineers, system analysts, or IT specialists.

Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED)

The BTVTED program trains future educators to teach technical-vocational subjects such as ICT, Home Economics, or Industrial Arts. Graduates can become licensed TVET instructors or trainers in public and private institutions.
