Mission

PHILTECH shall emerge as one of the educational institutions in the Philippines that can provide academic excellence, appropriate technology and produce graduates who are globally competitive and shall serve as pillar in this challenging world.

Vision

Philippine Technological Institute of Science Arts and Trade, Inc. (PHILTECH) is dedicated to give quality education to the development of every Filipino. It aims to hone students who are values oriented and physically, academically, socially, and spiritually committed to the achievement of life-long learning and service to the nation.

School History

The Philippine Technological Institute of Science Arts and
Trade Inc. (PHILTECH) was founded in 2010 in Tanay, Rizal,
by a diverse group of eleven professionals, including engineers, IT experts, and educators.
Starting as a non-stock, non-profit institution, it initially offered two-year
technical programs in Information Technology, Hotel and Restaurant
Services, and Business Outsourcing Management. By 2013, its first graduates completed their studies, supported by TESDA-Rizal accreditation, and the school expanded with new branches in Sta. Rosa, Laguna, and General Mariano Alvarez (GMA), Cavite, serving over 1,160 students by 2013–2014.
Committed to fostering global success through accessible education, PHILTECH prioritizes technical-vocational training, academic excellence, and skill development. As a patriotic, non-sectarian institution, it focuses on empowering Filipino youth to become productive citizens. With a dynamic faculty and a mission centered on community service, PHILTECH remains dedicated to molding future leaders while upholding its founders’vision of blending innovation, education, and social responsibility.
